The Story Behind GrihSeva

In the bustling city of Gurgaon, finding a reliable electrician or plumber used to be a matter of luck. We realized that while there were many skilled workers and many households in need, there was no bridge of trust and professionalism connecting them.

GrihSeva was started to solve this local problem. We are a community-first platform that empowers local artisans and service providers while giving homeowners peace of mind with vetted, high-quality doorstep services.
